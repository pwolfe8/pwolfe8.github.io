General Privacy

What Information We Collect From You
We collect your email & password in order for you to have an account on our server and log back in to gather your data. We do not share this data with anyone else. It is used purely for a login to grab a secure token and that’s it.

What Kind of Information is Collected From Your Child?

We collect touch data on your child based on the choices we present them.
This information is tied to the parent’s account and used to present progress and their child’s interests to the parent. It may also used anonymously in-aggregate to establish how a parent’s child is doing compared to the others of a similar group.

Data Types

There are two types of data that may be collected. The first one is called personal data. This is personally identifiable information that identifies a user as an individual. ConvexMind may collect personal data that parents voluntarily provide on the website. The second type is non personal data which doesn’t directly identify an individual or which may have been personal information but has had the personally identifiable information removed. ConvexMind may collect non personal information about the use of the website and apps to help us improve our services.

Data Retention

There are two types of data we retain: parent data & child data. Parent data is data used to login to our app by the parent or legal guardian of the child using the app. This data can be removed by contacting support@convexmind.io. Child data is game progress data associated with the child’s account under the parent’s account. This data can be deleted from the app by the parent. After deletion, child data will be anonymized and moved to our aggregate statistic database within a reasonable timeframe.
